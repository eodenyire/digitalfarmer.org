About Us

The 21st Century Digital Farmer Program is an initiative of Mucho Mangoes Ltd, a social enterprise that was born out of a passion and the desire to eliminate farm produce waste, losses, and poverty, in the Kenyan Mango farming industry.

The program prouds itself in integrating Rural African Farming Communities into ICT, Promoting an Inclusive Global Digital Society!

It is unacceptable that Rural Farming Communities are excluded from the Global Digital Society!

We believe that if rural African farmers can use the internet, they will be in a position to Interact with online market platforms, search for agricultural production information, learn new production techniques and technologies, solve problems in their farms and improve their production.
