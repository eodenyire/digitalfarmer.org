                               Our Impact

Since 2015, we have been able to train 482 farmers on pre and post harvest skills to assist them mass produce better quality mango fruits among other horticultural products.We have also trained a total of 361 people consisting of rural smallholder farmers, rural smallscale traders and rural youths on digital literacy, internet proficiency and smart phone proficiency, passing them through a three months ICT training.
