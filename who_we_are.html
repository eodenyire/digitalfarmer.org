Who we are

Our Purpose

We are committed in helping to Integrate Rural African Farming Communities into ICT, connecting people, enabling them to access information through the internet, Connecting them to online market platforms, increasing their knowledge base in agricultural production and other sphere, and thus eventually increasing their productivity, personal incomes, and spur Rural Economic Development. .

Our Mission

To Integrate Rural African Farming Communities into ICT by making ICT Skills Training the most affordable and conveniently accessible where they are!

Our Vision

The Vision of the 21st Century Digital Farmer Program is: An Inclusive Global Digital Society that serves everyone through sharing the benefits of ICTs equitably! .

Our Values

The following values drive our thoughts, words and actions:

  • People First- We are driven by the Change we want to see, and the Impact that Change Instigates in the World.
  • We uphold Honesty and Integrity. What we say, and what we do, equals to one and the same!
  • Innovation and Professionalism.
