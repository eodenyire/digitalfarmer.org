Our Approach

The 21st Century Digital Farmer Program uses Solar Powered Mobile ICT centres to provide 3 months ICT and Agricultural Production skills training to farming Communities in Rural Kenya. The training is conveniently delivered at the farmers location, using a flexible 2 hour per day model, to allow the farmers to attend to their farms and other social economic activities.

Training Sessions

We currently have three training sessions. The 1st session starts at exactly 8:00AM and ends at 10:00AM. The 2nd session starts at 10:30AM and ends at 12:30PM.The last session starts at 2:00pm and ends at exactly 4:00PM
